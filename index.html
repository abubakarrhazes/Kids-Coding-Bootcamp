Meet Our CEO

I am Umar Idris, a social entrepreneur and a youth activist. I am the coordinator of the Yali Network Sokoto. Co-founder/CEO of The 21st Century Entrepreneurs Hub. ( www.21stcehub.com.ng ) A leading platform that inspire, empower and promote young entrepreneurs by connecting them with opportunities, resources and building their capacities to leverage Digital skills to develop and manage their enterprises. My goal is to contribute in building the next generation of young entrepreneurs and reducing the unemployment rate particularly in North Western Nigeria through entrepreneurship. Also I'm the Founder Caliphate Digital Marketing. Chairman NYCC (National Youth Chambers of Commerce) Sokoto State Chapter. Member #NotTooYoungToRun Movement Sokoto. Member SAGE (Students For The Advancement Of Global Entrepreneurship) Sokoto State Chapter.
